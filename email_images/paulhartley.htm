LEASE-DESK is not a leasing company or a broker.
It is the ultimate Portfolio Management web-based solution for equipment vendors.
Designed by Proctor Consulting UK, Lease-Desk will improve your leasing data management and generate quality sales leads from live customer agreements.


LEASE-DESK is available on a subscription basis, providing vendors with full visibility of their leasing portfolio. It runs completely independently from your chosen leasing providers. The result is total transparency of every agreement’s information, allowing you to maximise all the potential profit from existing leases and current prospects. You wont need to wait for information from the leasing companies because you’ll already know it, automatically. You are in control.


LEASE-DESK , in addition to being ideal for vendors, is also of great benefit to distributors, providing channels with the best possible tool to assist them to sell into their client base.
